About Our Club

Building Champions Since 2023

Chancity Sports Club is dedicated to promoting Kabaddi at the grassroots level in Karnataka. We believe in nurturing talent, building character, and creating opportunities for aspiring athletes to shine on the national stage.

Our mission is to make Kabaddi accessible to everyone while maintaining the highest standards of training and sportsmanship.
