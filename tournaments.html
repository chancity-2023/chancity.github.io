Important Information

Tournament Rules

Team Composition

  • 12 players per team (7 starting + 5 substitutes)
  • All players must have valid Aadhaar ID proof
  • Team manager/coach required

Match Format

  • Standard Kabaddi rules (AKFI approved)
  • 40 minutes per match (2 halves of 20 min)
  • 5 minutes break between halves
  • Extra time in case of tie (5 minutes)

Equipment & Kit

  • Team jerseys with numbers mandatory
  • Proper sports shoes required
  • Knee pads and ankle support recommended
  • No jewelry or accessories allowed

Conduct & Fair Play

  • Umpire decisions are final
  • Zero tolerance for misconduct
  • Teams must report 30 min before match
  • Fair play awards for best conduct
