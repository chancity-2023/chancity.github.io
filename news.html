January 2024 | Featured

Tournament Registration Now Open!

The Chancity Open Kabaddi Tournament registration is now officially open! We invite all Kabaddi teams from schools, colleges, and clubs across Karnataka to participate in this prestigious annual event. Early bird registration is available for a limited time.

This year's tournament promises to be bigger and better than ever, with enhanced facilities, increased prize money, and exciting additions to the format. Don't miss your chance to be part of this incredible sporting event!

Recent News

Match reports, highlights, and club updates

January 15, 2024

New Training Programs Launched

We are excited to announce the launch of new training programs for junior athletes. The programs focus on fundamental skills, physical conditioning, and mental preparation for competitive Kabaddi.

Training sessions will be held every weekday from 4 PM to 6 PM. Interested students should contact the sports department for enrollment.

December 28, 2023

Regional Championship Victory!

Chancity Sports Club secures a stunning victory at the Regional Kabaddi Championship! Our senior team demonstrated exceptional teamwork and skill, defeating the defending champions in a thrilling final match.

Congratulations to all players and coaches for this remarkable achievement. Ready To Fight!

December 20, 2023

Summer Training Camp 2024 Announced

Mark your calendars! Our annual Summer Training Camp will be held in May 2024. This intensive residential camp is open to aspiring Kabaddi players from all over Karnataka.

Limited spots available. Registration details will be announced soon on this page and our social media channels.

December 15, 2023

Players Selected for State Team

We are proud to announce that three of our senior players have been selected to represent Karnataka in the upcoming National Kabaddi Championship! This is a testament to the quality of training at Chancity Sports Club.

We wish them the best as they compete at the national level.

December 5, 2023

Upgraded Training Facilities

Thanks to support from the Karnataka Sports Federation and our school administration, we have upgraded our training facilities with a new standard-size Kabaddi mat and improved gymnasium equipment.

These improvements will help our athletes train at the highest level.

November 25, 2023

Junior Team Wins Inter-School Tournament

Our junior team has won the District Inter-School Kabaddi Tournament! The young warriors displayed outstanding skills and determination, making us all proud.

Special thanks to our junior team coach for nurturing these talented athletes.
