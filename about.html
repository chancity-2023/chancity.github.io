About Chancity

Building champions through dedication, discipline, and teamwork since 2023.

Our Mission

Empowering Athletes, Building Champions

Chancity Sports Club was founded with a singular vision: to make professional Kabaddi training accessible to aspiring athletes across Karnataka. We believe that talent exists everywhere, but opportunities are rare.

Our mission is to identify, nurture, and develop Kabaddi talent from grassroots levels, providing world-class coaching, modern facilities, and competitive opportunities that help athletes reach their full potential.

Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Greatness

Founded in 2023 at G.H.P.S Channasandra, Bengaluru, Chancity Sports Club started with a small group of passionate Kabaddi enthusiasts and a dream to create something special.

Today, we've grown into one of Karnataka's most respected Kabaddi training centers, with over 500 trained players, multiple tournament victories, and a reputation for developing champions who compete at state and national levels.
